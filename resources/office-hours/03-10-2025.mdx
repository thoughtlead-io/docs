Join us weekly for **Office Hours** to discuss all things Thoughtly. [RSVP here](https://thoughtly.com/office-hours) to be notified about upcoming sessions.

#### Product Feedback and Troubleshooting [00:05:00]

- A user expressed difficulty with the system disconnecting before call completion and requested step-by-step beginner videos for better understanding. 
- Suggestions included watching platform-specific generic demos on YouTube to gauge market interest.
- Recommended pricing strategy emphasized higher rates due to regional demand but also encouraged smaller client bases for manageability. 
- Users plan to leverage Thoughtly for small businesses in Chamber of Commerce meetings.

#### Rapid-Fire Questions and Product Update [00:08:00]

- Announcement of a new Thoughtly version, V1.8, (previously labeled as V1.5) with improved conversational capabilities.
- V1.8 improves from version 1.5 by being more conversational and integrating advanced outcomes and mid-call actions.
- Discussion on the underlying language models, prompting questions about potential industries' applicability.
- In-depth conversation about the economics of building an agency with Thoughtly, with a focus on cost structures and pricing strategies.

#### Thoughtly Agent Deployment and Agency Building [00:15:00]

- Agency builders' pricing structures were analyzed, highlighting no extra costs outside of voice minute usage.
- The introduction of an eventual white-label dashboard, designed for seamless agency integration.
- Concerns about how an integrated dashboard might support extensive client bases using sub-accounts effectively.
- Insights into SMS and voice call integration for unified communication features.

#### Thoughtly Agent Insights and Configuration [00:25:00]

- Discussion on the updated product roadmap, notably the introduction of mid-call actions, enhanced text integration, and the white-label feature for agency builders.
- Users were guided toward current documentation and community resources for additional assistance.
- Challenges mentioned about Google Sheets integration due to new approval protocols, indicating temporary unavailability.
- Expanded opportunities for lead generation discussed, specifically enterprise applications and industry-specific configurations.

#### Technical Q&A and Platform Utilization [00:30:00]

- Inquiries into WhatsApp integration due to its popularity in European markets, with upcoming Meta collaboration.
- Confirmation on upgrading agent architecture to ensure better handling of interaction nodes and logical pathways.
- Feedback on conversational personalization models to suit marketing and lead conversation improvements.

#### Conversational Agent Enhancement [01:00:00]

- Users were briefed on enhancing personality and interactivity for conversational agents, including using prompts for enrichment.
- Emphasized strategic agent model enhancements to highlight user information carefully.
- Product demonstration with thoughtly agent configurations, including shared insights on setup, conversation initiation, and call flow.
- The future release plans to give insight into agent personalities, voice configurations, and agent presentation autonomy.

#### API, Automation, and Cost Queries [01:30:00]

- The API's extensive potential was acknowledged, with emphasis on expected future enhancements to further streamline operation workflows.
- Users were informed about details regarding data limit handling and architectures for large data flows.
- Trouble-shooting steps were discussed for pricing models and voice recognition setups on Thoughtly.
- Suggestions arose for more personalized interaction algorithms to increase relevancy in conversational agents.

#### After-Call Support and Interactive Session Wrap-Up [02:45:00]

- Final session focused on maximizing productivity with API calls and leveraging automations for advanced workflows.
- Conversations explored the potential impacts of solution scalability on client interactions, including handling call volumes and data persistency issues.
- A decision to leverage community platforms like Discord for better workflow management and collaboration.
