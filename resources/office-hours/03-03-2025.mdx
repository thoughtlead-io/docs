Join us weekly for **Office Hours** to discuss all things Thoughtly. [RSVP here](https://thoughtly.com/office-hours) to be notified about upcoming sessions.

#### Feedback on Reddit for Thoughtly [00:05:00]

- The team discussed the potential benefits of using Reddit as a platform for engaging with Thoughtly customers and the advantages of asynchronous communication.
- A member shared their experience with using Reddit to drive business by answering questions and clearing up misinformation in their industry.
- Concerns were raised about Reddit's unfamiliarity compared to other communication tools like Slack, with suggestions to allow time for the community to grow comfortable with the platform.

#### Limiting Interactions and Conditional Navigation [00:15:00]

- An attendee asked about limiting conversation lengths in Thoughtly's AI interactions, noting current difficulties due to AI's poor time management.
- A feature `call.duration` was mentioned, currently unavailable but expected soon, which should allow enforcing time limits in sessions.
- Upcoming feature `conditional navigation` will give more programmatic control to enforce exit conditions like time limits deterministically, expected around mid-April.

#### Audio Start Issues and Call Integration Discussion [00:25:00]

- Concerns about call start timing were discussed; some customers report conversations starting mid-call.
- The team is working on a feature that lets the AI wait for the user to speak first to ensure the call is properly connected.
- Participants discussed integration with various calendar and meeting tools like MS Bookings and Teams, addressing potential complications with call initiations and confirmations.

#### 1.8 Product Version Updates [00:40:00]

- Challenges with identifying product versions were raised, including suggestions for more visible versioning within the interface.
- A conversation highlighted the importance of ongoing training and the role of video libraries for user education on new features.
- The potential for a content creator program was discussed where users could create educational YouTube videos on Thoughtly processes.

#### 10DLC A2P Registration and Strategy [00:45:00]

- Registration for A2P 10DLC was highlighted as a complex process that users had questions about, including video documentation as a potential solution.
- Users requested clear strategies for small business accounts using a shared pool of resources for multiple clients under A2P registrants.
- A detailed document by an expert was shared as a helpful guide for navigating A2P 10DLC processes, providing the latest requirements and procedures.

#### Tech Stack and Integration Tools [01:05:00]

- Extensive discussion on using tools like N8N for automation and integrations with Thoughtly, especially to fill gaps where direct integrations (e.g., Google Sheets) are unavailable.
- Some users shared the significance of having customizable and scriptable nodes to enhance the flexibility of automation workflows in Thoughtly.

#### Industry Landscape and Competition [01:25:00]

- A conversation covered concerns about rapidly advancing AI technologies and staying competitive in a fast-paced industry.
- The dialogue shifted towards brand differentiation, highlighted by the unique features and interpretations that Thoughtly offers versus competitors.
- Shared thoughts on the changing landscape and strategies for web design and AI agencies echoing past market shifts in fields like SEO and web hosting.

#### AI Augmented Tools and Cloud Infrastructure Discussion [01:40:00]

- Users shared experiences with different cloud service providers and infrastructure for hosting AI and automation processes.
- Shared insights on effective server use and cost-efficient configurations for handling resource-intensive AI tasks.
- This segment included advice on using specific server providers and configurations to optimize for computational efficiency.

#### Community Initiatives and Networking [02:10:00]

- Ideas for organizing separate group calls among community members to deepen discussions on technical implementations and workflows.
- Discussions highlighted the importance of community support, knowledge-sharing, and collaboration to tackle common application challenges.
- The dialogue ended with suggestions for setting up regular group engagements, possibly leveraging time slots that work across different time zones.

Note: This summary excluded initial introductions and general pleasantries as directed.
