Join us weekly for **Office Hours** to discuss all things Thoughtly. [RSVP here](https://thoughtly.com/office-hours) to be notified about upcoming sessions.

#### Reddit Integration Feedback [00:05:00]

- Discussion on the use of Reddit as a platform for community engagement for Thoughtly.
- Mixed opinions about using Reddit vs. Slack for communication, with some attendees expressing preference for proprietary forum software.
- Some attendees, like Greg, shared positive experiences, emphasizing the business advantages and customer engagement possibilities on Reddit.
- Suggestions for leveraging Reddit as a strategic platform for thought leadership and customer interaction.

#### Product Implementation and Business Use Cases [00:10:00]

- Greg discussed how he aims to implement Thoughtly in his business, focusing on creating an automated notary service.
- Potential for automations in handling FAQs about the notary business using Thoughtly.
- The idea of using Thoughtly to replace standard website interactions with a more dynamic setup using webhooks and voice calls.
- Discussion about integration with Persona for identity validation and the benefits of using Thoughtly for international clients.

#### Feature Discussion and Troubleshooting: Call Timing and Webhooks [00:15:00]

- Tony K. raised concerns about AI's challenge with timekeeping during calls, questioning the effectiveness of using call duration variables in V1.8.
- Brief on upcoming features to address these issues, including conditional navigation based on call duration and accurate call start detection.
- Encounter with an issue where calls start mid-conversation, with Twilio's connection event reliability in question.
- Review of webhook issues where delays cause misinterpretation by AI, and proposed resolutions were shared.

#### API Updates and Use Case for Objective Nodes [00:20:00]

- Discussion on API updates where Tony requested new features and ensured current endpoints' stability during updates.
- Consideration of the reintroduction of the "objective node" to allow a blend of linear and conversational workflows, enhancing flexibility in workflow design.
- Detailed explanation of benefits for using objectives in conversations to maintain linear dialogue for specific use cases, like appointment bookings.

#### Support for International Phone Numbers and Texting [00:25:00]

- Support queries regarding enabling Indian phone numbers and texting within the Thoughtly application.
- Explanation of the process to get an Indian phone number and checking carrier capabilities, highlighting dependencies on Twilio and Telnyx.

#### Voice Quality and Platform Recommendations [00:30:00]

- Users reported stuttering issues with voices from 11 Labs and Carthesia, affecting demo quality.
- Recommendations to switch to alternative voices if experiencing issues, particularly with V1.8 updates.

#### Platform Integrations: Calendars and APIs [00:35:00]

- Discussion on calendar application integrations, including the use of Tidycal, cal.com, and other API integrations for booking automation.
- Comparison of various calendar apps and their suitability for different business models, especially in managing multi-agent settings.
- Mention of AppSumo deals and appropriate strategies for selecting calendar applications based on ease of use and API availability.

#### Educational Resources and Training for Thoughtly [00:40:00]

- Interest in creating a comprehensive video library for training and onboarding users.
- Proposal for a creator program to encourage community-generated content for educational purposes about using Thoughtly, enhancing resources such as video tutorials.

#### A2P 10DLC Compliance Process [00:45:00]

- Frustration with the complex and opaque A2P 10DLC registration process was articulated.
- Suggested creation of a complete guide or video to streamline the process, with warnings about common pitfalls and effective strategies for approval.
- Sharing of resources from well-informed community members and experts to aid others in 10DLC compliance.

#### Final Discussions and Community Coordination [02:10:00]

- Agreement on forming a technical discussion group outside of standard meetings for in-depth technical and business strategy discussions.
- Importance of community networking and resource sharing emphasized, demonstrating strong camaraderie amongst participants.
- Plans to continue detailed discussions and support through an organized email group or chat platform to facilitate ongoing collaboration.
