Join us weekly for **Office Hours** to discuss all things Thoughtly. [RSVP here](https://thoughtly.com/office-hours) to be notified about upcoming sessions.

#### Reddit Community [00:05:00]

- A discussion was held about the use of Reddit as a community platform for Thoughtly users, comparing its advantages to Slack and proprietary forum software.
- Some participants expressed skepticism about Reddit's effectiveness, preferring Slack for its synchronous communication abilities.
- Greg shared positive experiences using Reddit for business, highlighting its potential to drive business and increase visibility.
- The general consensus was to continue using Reddit due to its open nature and accessibility.

#### Notary Services and Thoughtly [00:10:00]

- Greg shared his experience using Thoughtly in his online notary business and discussed plans to expand its use for customer interactions.
- He explained using Reddit to answer public questions and educate people about his services, which generates business leads.
- Greg shared plans to implement Thoughtly for virtual customer interactions and automate parts of the notary process, increasing efficiency and engagement.

#### AI Interaction Limitations and Improvements [00:15:00]

- Tony K asked about setting limits on AI interactions, indicating challenges with the call duration variable in the current 1.8 version of Thoughtly.
- Torrey confirmed that some features, such as conditional navigation, will become available soon to address such issues.
- Discussions included future capabilities for controlling AI calls better, to create structured demos and avoid overly long interactions.

#### Technical Issues with Call Handling [00:20:00]

- Tony K experienced issues with calls connecting mid-conversation, creating confusion during demos.
- Torrey suggested this may be a Twilio issue, and they are working on a feature where the AI will wait for the person to speak first before starting the conversation.

#### API and Webhook Improvements [00:25:00]

- Tony K discussed issues with webhooks and the potential for interruptions when data is returned.
- The conversation touched on upcoming API updates that will include adding new contacts without affecting existing endpoints, assuring users of a smooth transition with minimal disruption.

#### 10DLC Registration Insights [00:40:00]

- Jamil raised concerns about the 10DLC registration process, which can complicate business operations, seeking improved guidance.
- Discussion covered the complexities and challenges involved, with suggestions to create a comprehensive guide or video to assist with the 10DLC process.
- Steven shared a resourceful document related to 10DLC, highlighting the importance of having accurate, updated information to navigate these registrations successfully.

#### Calendar Integration Tools [01:00:00]

- Various calendar tools and integrations were discussed, including TidyCal, Zcal, and the potential future integration with Thoughtly.
- Participants compared the strengths and weaknesses of different calendar APIs, focusing on features needed for multi-user and client scheduling.
- The consensus leaned toward integrating with existing scheduling software to leverage their superior capabilities in handling complex scheduling requirements.

#### AI and Automation Tools Discussion [01:15:00]

- The group engaged in a broad discussion on new AI and automation tools such as N8N and their potential to enhance business operations.
- Shane and Tony highlighted the power of using N8N for automation, fostering an ecosystem that enhances Thoughtly interactions through APIs.
- Innovative ideas for combining AI with automation were shared, indicating a trend toward using these technologies to increase productivity and create new business opportunities.

#### Community Collaboration [02:10:00]

- Participants expressed interest in forming a focused group to continue discussions and collaboration on technical projects outside of the main Thoughtly office hours.
- Ideas exchanged included ways to leverage collective knowledge for personal projects and business solutions, enhancing the value of shared experiences and learning within the Thoughtly community.
